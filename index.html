COUGAR

Cougar also known as Mountain lion is the world's fourth largest wildcat after lions , tigers and jaguars.

Scientific Classification

    >
  • Domain : Eukaryota
  • Kingdom: Animalia
  • Phylum: Chordata
  • Class: Mammalia
  • Order: Carnivora
  • Suborder: Feliformia
  • Family: Felidae

    • Cougar are stealthy predators,hunting at night and often lying in wait for prey or silently stalking it before pouncing from behind and delivery a lethal bite to the spinal cord Typically they prey on deer,it also feed on smaller animals,even insects, when necessary Cougars are the most widely distributed land mamals in the western Hemisphere.

    Lastly, Cougar live about 10 to 20 years in the wild and over 20 years in captivity
